Ibyerekeye TNI

Umuyoboro mpuzamahanga w'abasemuzi (TNI) ni urusobe rw'abasemuzi kandi yahumekeye abantu bemeza ko imirimo n'ubutumwa bwa Pasiteri Chris Oyakhilome arahari kandi aragerwaho mururimi rwunvikana neza n'abantu. Usibye guhindura, Umuyoboro uharanira gukwirakwiza ukuri kwimana ubutumwa bwiza bwa Yesu bwatanze binyuze mumahuriro atandukanye - no mundimi nziza byumvikanyweho nabantu.

Kuva Rhapsody of Realities Translations hamwe numudage wambere Ubuhinduzi mu 2002, 'Intumwa Umumarayika' nk'urumuri rwinshi, yatangiye urugendo rwa gukora ku buzima, umuntu umwe icyarimwe mururimi bumva neza.

Rero, hamwe nishyaka ryo kugera mumiryango yose nururimi, umumarayika wintumwa yahinduwe mundimi zindi
